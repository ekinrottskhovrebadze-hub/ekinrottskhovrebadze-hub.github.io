I'm a Creative Director and Head Screenwriter with over a decade of experience shaping some of Georgia's most-watched television and digital content. From flagship late-night talk shows on national broadcasters to a YouTube series that broke 200 million views in 8 months — I build narratives that resonate, and teams that deliver.

My work lives at the intersection of sharp writing, data-driven strategy, and creative leadership. I've managed production departments of 12+ specialists, run high-volume writers' rooms, and collaborated with directors, choreographers, and brands to create content that earns real attention.
